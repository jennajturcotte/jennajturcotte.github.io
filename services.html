Services

Front-End Development

After helping design my graduating year's yearbook in high school, I came to love visual design. I enjoy trying new design styles and making mockups for portfolios, websites, and mobile apps. Web design is something I am very passionate about.

Graphic Design

Much like front-end development, I also have a strong love for graphic design. Although I find it a bit of a challenge at times, I'm constantly learning new techniques and improving my work as a designer.

Coding/Web Development

While I find learning how to code websites extremely useful and interesting, it is also a huge frustration. But hard work always pays off in the end, which is why I continue to try and learn more and more advanced coding.

Content Management Systems

CMS is an extremely useful tool when working with clients. I found it particularly interesting because you can style your website how you want, but without the hassle of knowing how to code. This gives the user easy access to the back-end of their website, as they are able to update information on their own.

Photography

Photography is something I have enjoyed since my last year of high school. During my first year of college, I learned to really refine my skills and now photography is a skill I consider myself very good at, rather than just a hobby. I love taking nature photos, but I would really like the opportunity to do more photoshoots for portraits.

Video Editing

Even though I love photography, I find film/videography very different and at times, a little difficult. Don't get me wrong, though. Videography can be a lot of fun too. But my interest really peaks at the chance to do editing. Taking raw footage and turning it into a well-crafted story is what makes me happy.
